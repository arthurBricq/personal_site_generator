# Pace Buddy

A web app that provides personalized coaching advice powered by an LLM.

I built this app because I was tired of copy-pasting my strava data into ChatGPT. You have an AI agent that 
automatically gets all of your Strava data into its context, and that has read access onto your activities. 

Ask the coach all of the questions that you want. It will give you personalized advice based on your past activities.

Live at [pace-buddy.fly.dev](https://pace-buddy.fly.dev). If you want an invite code, please contact me.
